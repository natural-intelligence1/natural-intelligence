# Natural Intelligence — Project Brief

Natural Intelligence (NI) is an integrative-health platform that turns a member's symptoms, intake answers, and biomarker results into a clear, personalised health picture and a credible path forward. The platform is not a clinical service; it is a system for understanding health across time and connecting members with practitioners when human judgement is needed.

## Standing decisions

- NI is not a clinical service; it must not perform direct mental-health risk assessment.
- AI-generated artefacts (body story, synopsis) must quote back the member's signature question.
- Practitioner workspace surfaces clinical context (sex, free-text clinical notes) but never religion.
- All cross-table reads happen via SECURITY DEFINER RPCs with internal RLS checks.

## Audience

Members (general public, self-served), practitioners (licensed integrative health professionals), and operations (NI staff who triage cases).
