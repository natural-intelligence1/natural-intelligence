# Postpartum thyroiditis

Inflammation of the thyroid that can develop in the first year after pregnancy, often producing a transient hyperthyroid phase followed by a hypothyroid phase. Many women recover fully; a subset remain hypothyroid and require ongoing thyroid hormone support.

## Common presentation

- Fatigue that doesn't resolve with rest
- Cold intolerance, brain fog, dry skin
- Disrupted sleep architecture
- Mood changes — particularly anxiety in the hyperthyroid phase, low mood in the hypothyroid phase

## Functional medicine view

Postpartum is a period of significant hormonal, nutrient, and stress-axis depletion. Recovery quality matters more than the calendar date. Look at iron stores, B12, vitamin D, and HPA-axis function alongside thyroid panel.
